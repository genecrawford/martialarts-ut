What is MartialArtsUT?

MartialArtsUT is a free, comprehensive directory of every martial arts school in Utah. Whether you're looking for a karate dojo in Salt Lake City, a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu academy in West Valley City, or a taekwondo school near Provo, we're building the one place to find, compare, and connect with martial arts training across the entire state.

🔍

Search by Style or Location

Find schools by martial art style, city, zip code, or county. Filter by what matters to you — from beginner-friendly programs to competition training.

✅

Verified School Profiles

Verified Pro schools have been claimed and actively updated by their owners. You'll see photos, class schedules, instructor info, and program details you can trust.

Book Trial Classes

Many schools offer free trial classes. Find them on our directory and sign up directly — no phone calls, no guessing which schools welcome beginners.

Martial Arts Styles in Utah

Utah has a thriving martial arts community spanning traditional and modern disciplines. From the precision of karate and the grappling techniques of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu to the striking power of Muay Thai and the competitive intensity of MMA, there's a style for every age, goal, and fitness level. Our directory covers all major martial arts disciplines taught across Utah, including karate, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, taekwondo, MMA, Muay Thai, judo, kickboxing, kung fu, Krav Maga, wrestling, aikido, hapkido, capoeira, and self-defense programs.

Kids & Family Martial Arts Programs

Looking for after-school martial arts programs, summer camps, or kids' classes in Utah? Our directory makes it easy for parents to find age-appropriate training near home or school. Verified Pro schools list their after-school programs, summer camps, and holiday camps with age ranges, schedules, and direct signup links. Martial arts training builds discipline, confidence, fitness, and focus in children — and Utah has hundreds of schools offering youth programs across the state.
